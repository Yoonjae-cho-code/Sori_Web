Listen to Your Voice,
Not the Mirror

Sori — 소리, an interactive mental wellness companion in Korean Sori, or sori, translates to sound. It is not a productivity app. No streaks, no scores, no measures.

Just somewhere to be, and be heard, and speak. There is one question per session: How are you right now? You select feeling, talk little or much as necessary, and Sori in some way echoes it back upon itself, a kind of faint reflection of whatever you came there with.

Made to suit the no-name cachetiers. Constructed with regard to the space between feeling and language.
